Homemade Oreo Ice cream

Ingredients

  • Oreo cookies 1 cup (crushed)
  • Heavy cream 2 cups
  • Whole milk 200 ml
  • Sugar Â¾ cup
  • Vanila extract 1-2 tsp

Buy These Ingredients Now

Cooking Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl put milk and sugar and whisk till sugar dissolve
  2. Pour heavy cream and vanilla extract into mixing bowl and fold with spatula.
  3. ake an airtight container and pour all the mixture cover and freeze for about 4 hours.
  4. Take out from freezer and mix the semi solid mixture and put again into freezer.
  5. Repeat again mix and now add crushed oreo cookies put ice cream in to freezer for about 7-8 hour till freeze well.
  6. Scoop out ice cream and decorate with oreo biscuits and enjoy.
